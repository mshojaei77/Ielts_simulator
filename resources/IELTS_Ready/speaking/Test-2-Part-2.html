IELTS Speaking Test 2

Part 2

Duration: 3-4 minutes | Format: Long turn (individual presentation)

Note: You have 1 minute to prepare and 2 minutes to speak. Take notes if you wish.

Candidate Task Card

Describe an academic achievement you are proud of

You should say:

  • What this achievement/experience was
  • When and where it happened
  • What challenges you faced
  • Why it was important to you

You will have to talk about the topic for one to two minutes. You have one minute to think about what you are going to say. You can make some notes to help you if you wish.
