IELTS Speaking Test 3

Part 1

Duration: 4-5 minutes | Format: Introduction and interview

Note: Answer the questions naturally and provide detailed responses. The examiner will ask follow-up questions based on your answers.

Topic: Health and fitness routines

  1. Can you tell me about your current studies or work?
  2. What aspects of health and fitness routines do you find most interesting?
  3. How has health and fitness routines changed in your country over recent years?
  4. What role does health and fitness routines play in your daily life?
