IELTS Speaking Test 2

Part 1

Duration: 4-5 minutes | Format: Introduction and interview

Note: Answer the questions naturally and provide detailed responses. The examiner will ask follow-up questions based on your answers.

Topic: Environmental awareness

  1. Can you tell me about your current studies or work?
  2. What aspects of environmental awareness do you find most interesting?
  3. How has environmental awareness changed in your country over recent years?
  4. What role does environmental awareness play in your daily life?
