IELTS Academic Writing Task 2
Time: 40 minutes | Minimum words: 250
Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programmes (for example working for a charity, improving the neighbourhood or teaching sports to younger children).
To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Task Requirements:

  • Write at least 250 words
  • Spend approximately 40 minutes on this task
  • Present a clear position on the topic
  • Support your arguments with relevant examples
  • Use a range of vocabulary and grammar
  • Organize ideas logically with clear paragraphs
  • Address the question directly

Suggested Essay Structure:

Introduction (50-60 words)
• Paraphrase the question
• State your clear position
• Outline main points
Body Paragraph 1 (80-100 words)
• First main argument
• Supporting explanation
• Specific example
• Link to next paragraph
Body Paragraph 2 (80-100 words)
• Second main argument OR counter-argument
• Supporting explanation
• Specific example
• Analysis of implications
Conclusion (40-50 words)
• Summarize main points
• Restate your position
• Final thought or recommendation

Key Vocabulary for this Topic:

Community service: voluntary work, civic duty, social responsibility, community engagement
Education: curriculum, academic development, extracurricular activities, holistic education
Benefits: character building, empathy, practical skills, social awareness
Challenges: time constraints, academic pressure, individual choice, implementation difficulties
