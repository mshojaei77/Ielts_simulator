IELTS Academic Writing Task 1
Time: 20 minutes | Minimum words: 150
Instructions:
The chart below shows the percentage of households in owned and rented accommodation in England and Wales between 1918 and 2011.

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.
Percentage of households in owned and rented accommodation in England and Wales
Year Owned accommodation (%) Rented accommodation (%)
1918 23 77
1939 32 68
1953 31 69
1961 42 58
1971 50 50
1981 56 44
1991 67 33
2001 69 31
2011 65 35

Key Requirements:

  • Write at least 150 words
  • Spend approximately 20 minutes on this task
  • Describe the main trends and patterns
  • Make comparisons between owned and rented accommodation
  • Include specific data from the chart
  • Use appropriate academic vocabulary
  • Organize your response with clear introduction, body, and conclusion
