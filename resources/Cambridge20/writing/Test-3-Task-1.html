Writing Task 1

Academic Writing Task

Time: 20 minutes

Minimum words: 150

Instructions

The diagram below shows the process for recycling plastic bottles.

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

Write at least 150 words.

Plastic Bottle Recycling Process

[Process diagram showing the recycling cycle]

  1. Plastic bottles are thrown away in recycling bins
  2. Bottles are collected by trucks and taken to recycling center
  3. Bottles are sorted by hand
  4. Bottles are compressed into blocks
  5. Blocks are crushed and washed
  6. Plastic is melted to produce pellets
  7. Pellets are heated to make raw material
  8. Raw material is used to produce new products (clothing, bottles, bags, pencils)
