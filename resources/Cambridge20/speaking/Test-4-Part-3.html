Test 4-Part 3

This part lasts 4–5 minutes. You will discuss abstract topics related to Part 2. Give detailed answers with explanations and examples.

Discussion Topic: Sports and competition

1. What are the benefits of playing sports?
2. Do you think competitive sports are good for children?
3. What is the difference between watching sports on TV and watching sports live?
4. Why do you think some sports are more popular than others?
5. Do you think professional athletes are paid too much money?
