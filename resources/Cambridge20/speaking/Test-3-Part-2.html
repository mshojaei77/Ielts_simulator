Test 3-Part 2

You will have 1 minute to prepare and then speak for 1-2 minutes on the topic below.

Describe a rule that you don't like

You should say:

  • what it is
  • why you don't like it
  • how others feel about the rule
  • and explain whether you've followed the rule
Preparation time: 1 minute
Speaking time: 1-2 minutes
