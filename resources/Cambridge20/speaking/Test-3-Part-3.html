IELTS Speaking Test - Part 3
Two-way Discussion · Abstract topics related to Part 2

Test 3-Part 3

This part lasts 4–5 minutes. You will discuss abstract topics related to Part 2. Give detailed answers with explanations and examples.

Discussion Topic: Rules and regulations

1. What kinds of rules do people have to follow in your country?
2. Are there any rules that you think should be changed?
3. Why do some people choose not to follow certain rules?
4. How important is it for children to learn about rules?
5. Do you think that rules will become stricter or more relaxed in the future?
