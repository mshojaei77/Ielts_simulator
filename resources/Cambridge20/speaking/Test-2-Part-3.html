Two-way Discussion · Abstract topics related to Part 2

Test 2-Part 3

This part lasts 4–5 minutes. You will discuss abstract topics related to Part 2. Give detailed answers with explanations and examples.

Discussion Topic: Problem-solving

1. Why is it important for children to learn problem-solving skills?
2. Do you think there are any differences between how children and adults solve problems?
3. How can schools encourage students to develop better problem-solving abilities?
4. Do you think technology helps or hinders people's problem-solving skills?
5. What role does creativity play in solving problems?
