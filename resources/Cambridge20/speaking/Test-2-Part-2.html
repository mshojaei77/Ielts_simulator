Test 2-Part 2

You will have 1 minute to prepare and then speak for 1-2 minutes on the topic below.

Describe a person who solved a problem in a smart way

You should say:

  • who this person is
  • what the problem was
  • how he/she solved it
  • and explain why you think he/she did it in a smart way
Preparation time: 1 minute
Speaking time: 1-2 minutes
