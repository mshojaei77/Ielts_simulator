IELTS Speaking Test - Part 2
Long Turn · Prep then speak · One slide per phase

Preparation (1 minute)

You have one minute to prepare your talk. You can make notes. Recording starts when you begin speaking.

Read the cue card on the next slide and plan your talk. Aim to speak for up to two minutes.

Describe a teacher who influenced you.
  • Who the teacher is and what they taught
  • What they were like
  • How they influenced you
  • Why you remember them
Speak continuously for up to two minutes. Try to structure your talk with a clear beginning, middle, and end.

Follow-up

Do you think teachers today face more challenges than in the past? Why?
